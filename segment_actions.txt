Champions

1. Provide VIP rewards such as exclusive discounts and early access to new products.
2. Encourage referrals and reviews by offering bonus loyalty points.
3. Offer premium membership programs to maintain engagement.

Loyal Customers

1. Send personalized product recommendations based on purchase history.
2. Offer loyalty bonuses or reward multipliers to strengthen retention.
3. Introduce subscription or repeat purchase incentives.

Big Spenders

1. Promote premium or high-value product bundles.
2. Provide special coupons for luxury or high-margin categories.
3. Invite them to exclusive premium customer tiers.

New Customers

1. Send a welcome email series with a first-purchase discount.
2. Highlight best-selling products to guide future purchases.
3. Use targeted ads to encourage a second purchase quickly.

Potential Loyalists

1. Offer limited-time promotions to increase repeat buying.
2. Suggest complementary products based on recent purchases.
3. Enroll them in loyalty programs with bonus points.

At Risk

1. Launch win-back campaigns with attractive discounts.
2. Send reminder emails showcasing new arrivals.
3. Collect feedback to understand why engagement dropped.

Hibernating

1. Send seasonal promotions to reactivate interest.
2. Highlight trending or discounted products.
3. Reduce marketing spend if there is no response.

Others

1. Test different marketing messages and offers.
2. Use discovery campaigns to explore their interests.
3. Monitor behavior for movement into key segments.
